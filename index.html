About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to filling critical digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

By emphasizing the crucial link between academic insight - powered by our UK university partnership - and real-world project deployment, we bridge the gap between theory and practical resilience.

Our Service Model

Train → Hire → Innovate → Deploy

Academy

Intensive training and bootcamps designed for both students and seasoned professionals looking to master digital risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing cutting-edge IP, future risk models, and robust AI governance frameworks for tomorrow's challenges.

Advisory Services

Helping modern organizations seamlessly implement critical frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Who We Serve

Our growing community consists of ambitious students, early-career professionals, and seasoned practitioners in vital sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure who are seeking advanced upskilling in digital and cyber risk.
